AlMansour
Al-Mansour’s villa is an architectural masterpiece that blends the grandeur of classical design with the openness of modern style. The main façade is adorned with graceful columns and elegant arches inspired by classical architecture, while clean lines and sharp angles introduce a contemporary touch that gives the villa a distinguished character. The villa features a spacious main entrance leading into a naturally lit hall, thanks to large floor-to-ceiling windows that balance light and ventilation. The interior layout is designed to ensure both comfort and privacy, with the grand living hall opening directly onto views of the garden and swimming pool.
